As a leading provider of gemstones and crystals, we take pride in offering specimens at the lowest prices possible. We are dedicated to serving the material needs of rock collectors, lapidary workers and of customers who value the metaphysical properties of stones.

We have been in business for 17 years in the Oak Grove neighborhood, and have gained a reputation for providing a wide range of affordable rocks, minerals and jewelry. In addition to stones, we offer statuary, music, funky clothing, incense, candles, essential oils, natural body care products, natural supplements and an assortment of gift items from around the globe.
